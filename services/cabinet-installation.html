How Cabinet Installation Works

A clear, three-step process from first contact to finished space.

1

Site Visit & Measurement

We assess the space, confirm rough openings, check for plumb walls, and map stud locations. You get a written quote covering labor and any materials needed.

2

Delivery Coordination

If you're supplying the cabinets, we coordinate delivery timing and inspect units on arrival — catching damage before installation begins, not after.

3

Installation & Walkthrough

Cabinets hung and leveled. Fillers and scribes cut. Doors adjusted. You walk through the completed space and sign off — we don't leave until you're satisfied.
