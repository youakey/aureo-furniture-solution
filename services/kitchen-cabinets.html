Our Installation Process

Three clear steps — from estimate to finished kitchen.

1

Measurement & Quote

We measure your kitchen on-site (or via your plans), confirm the cabinet layout, and provide a written quote. No hidden fees.

2

Cabinet Delivery & Staging

Cabinets arrive to your home. We stage them by run, inspect for damage, and confirm the installation sequence before starting.

3

Precision Installation

Level lines snapped. Cabinets hung, shimmed, and screwed to studs. Fillers cut. Doors and drawers adjusted. Site cleaned before we leave.
